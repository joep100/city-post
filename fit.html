How the gauge works

Everything travels by bike — so the only question is how big the bike's box is on your route.

Bigger box in the city centre

City-centre runs (Dublin 1–8) go by cargo bike — up to 80×60×60 cm, 30 kg. Greater Dublin (D9–24) is up to 40×30×30 cm, 8 kg. Flip the toggle on the gauge to check your route.

Three sizes, priced by fit

Small parcel, Parcel or Box — from €7.99. Pick the smallest and if it's a touch over, we simply bump it to the next tier and charge that rate. No surprises.

Too big? We split it.

If it won't fit either box, send it as two parcels — still same-day. Or if a Small parcel is a touch over, we simply move it up to the next tier and charge that rate.

The gauge covers size & weight only. We can't carry hazardous goods (batteries, aerosols, flammables) or restricted items (cash, alcohol, anything illegal). If in doubt, ask us first.
