Why the tight radius?

A small, dense map is the whole trick. Because every drop is inside ~12 km, one rider can chain several jobs on a single run instead of driving across a county for one parcel. That efficiency is what lets us deliver same-day — and hold the price at a flat €7.99. Go nationwide and you lose both.

Why cargo bikes in the centre?

Inside the canals the bike wins — bus lanes, no gridlock, park at the door — so it beats a van door-to-door, with zero emissions. Push past ~3 km and open road makes the motorbike quicker. Right vehicle for the distance, every time — and the same parcel sizes fit either way.
